Amara Hollow Bones — Archive
============================

This folder is a local archive of Amara Hollow Bones's Facebook page,
including posts, captions, and 951 photos.

HOW TO OPEN
-----------
1. Keep this entire folder together (do not move photos out of the photos/ folder)
2. Open "index.html" in any web browser (Firefox, Chrome, Safari, Edge)
   — just double-click it, or right-click → Open With → your browser

WHAT'S INSIDE
-------------
index.html          The main page — open this in your browser
photos/             All 951 photos from the page
README.txt          This file

NAVIGATING THE PAGE
-------------------
Posts tab    Chronological list of posts with their associated photos
             Scroll down past the dated posts for additional text posts & comments
Gallery tab  Full photo gallery — all photos in a browsable grid
             Click any photo to enlarge it; press Escape or click outside to close

NOTES
-----
Photos that could be matched to a post are shown together in the Posts view.
Some photos appear only in the Gallery (they were posted without captions
or their post text was not captured).
